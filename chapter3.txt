The Midnight Stalker

Edgar's presence in Havenbrook took a sinister turn as reports of a shadowy figure stalking the streets at midnight spread like wildfire. The townspeople lived in fear, afraid to venture out after dark. Whispers of an unknown danger echoed through the quiet streets, creating an atmosphere of paranoia. The town's sense of security crumbled, and nobody knew when or where the elusive psycho killer would strike next.
