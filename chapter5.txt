Climax
Detective Sarah Lawson's investigation into the silent vengeance led her to a chilling discovery. Edgar, consumed by personal vendettas, had orchestrated the disappearances as a form of twisted justice. In a haunting revelation, Sarah exposed the truth, leaving the town in a state of disbelief and sorrow. As the echoes of the vanished lingered, Havenbrook struggled to reconcile with the shadows of its past. The community, forever changed, faced the daunting task of healing wounds and rebuilding the bonds shattered by Edgar's vengeful path.
