Pre-Climax
Detective Sarah Lawson delved into the mysterious disappearances that plagued Havenbrook. As she connected the dots, a pattern emerged, leading her to Edgar's twisted sense of justice. Uncovering the motive behind the silent vengeance, Sarah faced the challenge of unmasking the killer's rationale while grappling with the realization that the victims were chosen for subjective reasons. The town, gripped by fear and uncertainty, looked to Sarah for answers, unaware that the revelation of Edgar's motive would only deepen the shadows looming over Havenbrook.
