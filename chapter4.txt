chapter 4: Pre-Climax
Detective Sarah Lawson, sensing the escalating fear in Havenbrook, intensified her investigation into the Midnight Stalker. The town's once peaceful atmosphere had transformed into a haunting landscape as residents barricaded themselves indoors after sunset. Sarah meticulously pieced together witness accounts and surveillance footage, hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive figure preying on the darkness. The race against time heightened as the shadows grew longer, and the town awaited answers to quell the rising panic.
